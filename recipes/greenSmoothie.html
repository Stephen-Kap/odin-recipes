Refresh & Reboot Green Smoothie

Description

This healthy smoothie is the perfect way to start your day. Not only is it nutritious and filling, it tastes great! This smoothie loaded with greens will give you the energy you need to make it through your day

Ingredients

  • half cup spinach
  • half of a pear, chopped
  • half of a lemon, squeezed
  • 1 cup Almond Breeze Original Almondmilk

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.
